Caltech is a highly trusted science and engineering institute in Pasadena, California, with an undergraduate acceptance rate of about 3.9%. It receives around 8,000 applications yearly. Applications use the application website and require a very good math and science records, as well as strong test scores, essays, and recommendations. Caltech has a high priority for the applicants STEM achievments and problem-solving. Tuition costs can be up to $100,000 NZDper year. Scholarships are available but are hard to get. Caltech is ideal for NZ students excelling in STEM subjects.
