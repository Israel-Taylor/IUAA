Known for its cutting-edge research, and exceptional academic environment, Caltech has an undergraduate acceptance rate of around 3.9%. Applications are submitted via the online application process. New Zealand applicants should present exceptional academic performance in maths and science, typically NCEA Level 3 with Excellence endorsements, or equivalent qualifications such as Cambridge A Levels. Need-based financial aid is available to international students, and Caltech meets 100% of demonstrated need, but merit scholarships are rare and difficult to earn.
