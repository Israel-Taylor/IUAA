Imperial College London is one of the best Universities in the UK and Globally. "ICL" specialises in Science, engineering, medicine, and business. It ranks consistently among the world's best for STEM relates subjects and is known for cutting-edge research, and innovation. ICL admits around 30% of undergraduate applicants from a pool of roughly 17,000 each year, making entry quite difficult.

Financial aid and scholarships are limited for non-UK students, but some merit-based scholarships are available, including department-specific awards and the President's Undergraduate Scholarship.
