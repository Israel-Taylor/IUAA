The University of Cambridge, one of the world's best known and trusted universities, it accepts about 21% of an average of around 20,000+ undergraduate applicants each year.

  • Applications are submitted using "UCAS" by the middle of October each year and requires difficult academic achievements (like as NCEA Excellence), course-specific admissions tests, and interviews.
  • Tuition fees for international students vary widely depending on the chosen course, typically between £25,000 and £60,000 per year or $50,000 to $100,000 nzd annually.
  • Cambridge offers some scholarships for international undergraduates, although these are limited and hard get.
